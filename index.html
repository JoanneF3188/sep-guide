Inside Learning

  • Participate in class
  • Do your homework
  • Refer to slides and google docs for tests or quizzes
  • Take notes on a google doc during class to put certain concepts in your own words
  • When presenting in class, just relax and take a deep breath. Be proud of your hard work. :)
  • Go to tutoring if you need help
  • Don't be late to class
    • "Early is on time" -Mr. Mueller
  • Make the most out of class trips. They are a great opportunity for networking.
  • In regards to projects,
    • "Done is better than perfect" -Mr. Mueller
    Make sure you meet all the project requirements and keep in mind the ways that you would want to further improve a project. Reflection is an important part of being a coder, and a person.

Outside Learning

  • Take advantage of opportunities such as internships or hackathons
      Some examples include:
    • GenTech
    • Pathfinders Program
    • Code Like a Girl
  • LOYO (Learn on your own)
  • If you have time I recommend making projects on your own to practice and to utilize coding in a way that interests you.

Useful Skills

  • Plan before you code. Psuedocode (Putting code or what you want to do/ what you want to happen in your own words/human language)
  • Listen in class and take notes on concepts or information you deem important on a Google doc
    • I find it useful to translate notes into my own words and not use complete sentences to better understand certain concepts
  • Communicate with your partners over text messaging or facebook messaging and try to divide the work evenly
  • When working on projects that only one partner submits, establish who will submit the project the night before
  • Do not give up. Perservere :)
  • Do not be discouraged by errors. Errors are awesome.

Resources

  • W3schools
  • Class Slides
  • Your teacher
  • Your peers
  • JSBin is useful when testing out code or playing around with a certain concept/piece of code
  • Code Academy is a great website to learn various languages or concepts
  • Google is your friend
