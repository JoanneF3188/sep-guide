Inside Learning

  • Participate in class
  • Do your homework
  • Refer to slides and google docs for tests or quizzes
  • Take notes on a google doc during class to put certain concepts in
  • When presenting in class, just relax and take a deep breath. Be proud of your hard work. :)
  • Go to tutoring if you need help
  • Don't be late to class
    • "Early is on time" -Mr. Mueller
  • Make the most out of class trips. They are a great opportunity for networking.
  • In regards to projects,
    • "Done is better than perfect" -Mr. Mueller
    Make sure you meet all the project requirements and keep in mind ways that you would want to further improve a project
